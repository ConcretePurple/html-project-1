Amritsr Restaurant

Sundos Al Nahda Building - Amman St - Al Nahda 2 - Dubai

Amritsr Restaurant specializes in authentic Punjabi cuisine, offering a diverse menu that highlights traditional dishes crafted with quality ingredients. Signature items like Amritsari kulcha and dal makhani are must-tries for visitors. The restaurant prides itself on its warm ambiance and excellent service, making it an ideal spot for families and gatherings. Additionally, Amritsr provides outdoor catering and boasts multiple branches in Dubai and Thailand. With a focus on fresh food and prompt delivery, Amritsr aims to create a memorable dining experience for all.
